Workout Routine

Upper Lower Push Pull Legs Split

This workout takes 5 days a week. You can either run through the split and have two
rest days or have the first section Upper Lower, rest then the second section push pull
legs. It all works the same.

Details of the workout

UPPER:

This workout would focus on the upper body

Exercise sets reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 4 8-12
Dumbbell Bench Press 4 6-8
Barbell Rows 4 6-8
Chin Ups 3 AMAP
Close Grip Bench Press 3 8-12
S1. Side raises 4 12-15
S2. Rear delt fly 4 12-15

Note: *AMAP(As Many As Possible),S1 S2 Superset 1 and 2
