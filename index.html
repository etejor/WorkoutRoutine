Workout Routine

Upper Lower Push Pull Legs Split

This workout takes 5 days a week. You can either run through the split and have two
rest days or have the first section Upper Lower, rest then the second section push pull
legs. It all works the same.

Details of the workout

UPPER:

This workout would focus on the upper body

Exercise sets reps Demonstration
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 4 8-12 How to Shoulder Press
Dumbbell Bench Press 4 6-8 How to DB Bench Press
Barbell Rows 4 6-8
Chin Ups 3 AMAP
Close Grip Bench Press 3 8-12
S1. Side raises 4 12-15
S2. Rear delt fly 4 12-15

Note: *AMAP(As Many As Possible),S1 S2 Superset 1 and 2

This workout would focus on the Lower body

This workout would focus on the push muscles: Chest and triceps, biceps

Exercise sets reps Demonstration

Although its a push day we would add biceps because the muscle would be fresh, and on the pull day we would focus on the back.
